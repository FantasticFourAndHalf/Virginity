History

Virginity Restaurant & Bar is a traditional European cuisine venue, serving the best dishes and drinks from across the range of European cuisines. A definitive neighborhood joint in Davis Square, Resto offers a laid-back dining experience in a casual setting to guests from all walks of life. Honest food and honest drinks set the foundation of our place's menu!
